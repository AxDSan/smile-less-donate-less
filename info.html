Smile More, Donate More :)

This lightweight extension redirects you to the smile.amazon.com mirror page every time you visit Amazon. Amazon Smile is Amazon's charity program to non-profit organizations. For every purchase through smile.amazon.com, Amazon donates 0.05% of the price of eligible items to the charity organization of your choice. Learn more at: https://smile.amazon.com/gp/chpf/pd/ref=smi_se_saas_lpd_spd
