Smile More, Donate More :)

See Your AmazonSmile Impact

This lightweight extension redirects you to the smile.amazon.com mirror page every time you visit Amazon. Amazon Smile is Amazon's charity program for non-profit organizations. For every purchase through smile.amazon.com, Amazon donates 0.05% of the price of eligible items to the charity organization of your choice.
Learn more at: AmazonSmile
